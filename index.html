***Cheesy Hot Pizza***

How make a Pizza ** MASTERCHEF JUNIOR 
                                                      
    
   
          Time to Start Cooking!                             

    
Step 1 : Prepare Oven and Dough:

    
Preheat the oven to 500 degrees Fahrenheit with a rack in the upper third of the oven. If you’re using a baking stone or baking steel, place it on the upper rack. Next prepare the dough for the pizza.


         
Step 2: The Cheesier the better:

        
Spread pizza sauce evenly over the two pizzas, leaving about 1 inch bare around the edges. Evenly distribute the spinach on top of the sauce, followed by the cheese (use all three cups if you want a cheesy pizza as shown here).

        

       
        
Step 3: Pizza Toppings:

        
Top the pizzas with artichoke, bell pepper, red onion, tomatoes, olives and almonds (if using). Feel Free to add any other toppings to match your taste.
   
   
           

          
           
Step 4: Its Baking Time!:
 
           
Bake pizzas individually on the top rack until the crust is golden and the cheese is golden and bubbly, about 10 to 12 minutes (or significantly less, if you’re using a baking stone/steel—keep an eye on it).
           


           
Step 5: Time to eat and Save!!!:
 
           

            Transfer pizzas to a cutting board and sprinkle with with fresh basil, red pepper flakes and Parmesan, if using. Slice and serve! Leftover pizza keeps well in the refrigerator for 4 days, or for several months in the freezer. Dig in and Enjoy with friends and family(or just yourslef)
            

            
Hope you enjoyed making and eating your delicious cheesy customizable Pizza!
